This Must Be It

2013

audio engineering/post-production for film

A short film directed by Trevor Iverson. This Must Be It, is a dark comedy about a young man named Oliver who lives a mundane life. When a childhood friend passes away, he is left in charge of disposing the remains.
As lead sound engineer and mixer, I worked close with Iverson to determine the score and tone of the film. During the scene in the woods, I used a variety of pitch shifting techniques, along with samples from the film to give a sense of reflection and unease to the viewer.

  • Creator & Director: Trevor Iverson
  • Sound Editor/Sound Mixer: Spencer Witter
