Collective Noise

2014

Project Management

Synthesthesia is a live electronic perfomance night at the Union Depot. With the city of St. Paul as the backdrop, audeince members are able to explore the bleeding edge of electronic music from artists who call the Twin Cities their home.

Shows ran through the summer 2014, under the umbrella of Bedlam Theatre (R.I.P).

  • Project Management: Spencer Witter
  • Participating Artists: John Keston, Lucas Melchior and Mike Hodnick
  • Poster Designer: Ethan Weise
